Call for Papers

The Pervasive Agentic Systems and Multimodal Foundation Models (PerAgent) workshop focuses on the critical systems-level challenges of transitioning from narrow, singletask edge AI to general-purpose, autonomous AI agents operating in physical environments. Currently, foundation models are trapped in the cloud due to massive computational requirements. This workshop explores how large-scale models such as Large Language Models (LLMs), Vision-Language Models (VLMs), and Multimodal Language Models (MLMs) can be compressed, decentralized, and securely integrated with pervasive sensor networks.

Topics of interest include but are not limited to:

  • Hardware-software co-design for edge agents
  • Decentralized and split/federated agentic learning
  • Multimodal sensor fusion for VLMs/MLMs
  • Trust, safety, and local overrides for physical actuation
  • Bandwidth-efficient distributed inference
  • Cross-domain agent adaptation algorithms
  • Agentic synchronization across IoT mesh networks

Submission instructions

Authors are invited to submit full papers that are unpublished and not under review elsewhere. All submissions will undergo a peer-review process by Program Commitee members in a single blind fashion.

All accepted papers will be published as part of the PerCom satellite events proceedings. Proceedings will be published by the IEEE and available online through IEEE Digital Library.

Submission deadline:December 7th, 2026

You can submit your paper on EDAS by clicking here

Important: each accepted paper requires a full PerCom registration and at least one author attending the workshop to present it (no registration is available for workshops only). Papers without a valid full registration or that are not presented in-person will be excluded from the proceedings.

Paper format

  • Workshop papers are to be no more than 6 pages (overall, i.e. including references). A maximum of 1 additional page may be purchased.
  • FORMAT: IEEE template, 10 pt, 2-column format (same template as the main conference)
